Our big idea: an application designed to bring people and food
together––a virtual “Potluck” coordinator. We want these
grub get-togethers to encourage self-made meals as well as
meaningful connection. The app facilitates face-to-face social
interaction by lowering the bar to entry of planning gatherings and
finding recipes. .

Potluck allows you to filter recipes by dietary restrictions,
preferences & allergies; this troubleshoots the complications of
accommodating multiple types of eaters. Potluck sends invites via
text to make coordinating appetizers, main dishes and courses
easy with sign-up sheets and suggestions.

features

-plan

See all of your event plans and details in one place. Find new invites & see who is going.

-calendar

Keep track of dates and schedule new events on a calendar that syncs with both Google & Apple devices.

-recipes

Search for recipes that will accmodate your entire group and filter by ingredients you have on hand.

-profile

Where you can share any dietary restrictions & stay updated on recent Potluck activity.
